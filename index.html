QA Engineer

I am a seasoned QA engineer with over 20 years of experience in quality assurance, dedicated to ensuring that software is robust, reliable, and meets high standards for performance and usability. Over the course of my career, I have worked on a range of projects, often being the first to establish and implement QA processes where none existed previously. I took full responsibility for creating detailed test designs and comprehensive test cases, ensuring that all potential issues were anticipated and thoroughly checked before release.


My most recent QA Engineer role was with Gravyty, based in Ra'anana. I was responsible for testing their Advance project, which involved comprehensive testing of credit card payment processes. This ensured that integrations with popular payment providers, such as Stripe and BBMS, were secure, reliable, and user-friendly. My work at Gravyty included both front-end testing to ensure a smooth user experience and back-end testing on a system built with Ruby on Rails.


I am well-versed in using automated testing tools, having utilized Selenium WebDriver (written in Python) and Cypress to streamline test execution and improve efficiency. With these tools, I have developed automated test suites that have significantly reduced manual testing time and helped identify potential issues earlier in the development cycle.

Technical Writing

With many years of experience as a technical writer, I bring a unique blend of technical insight and communication skills to documentation projects, particularly those in software development. I am fluent in English, and I have a B.A. in English from Brooklyn College.


My current work at Sony Semiconudctors Israel involves creating and editing technical documentation for our customer base. In this role, I am responsible for generating comprehensive, user-friendly materials that our customers can access easily in both digital and PDF formats. I also designed a dedicated website using Asciidoc, providing a seamless platform where documentation is available in multiple formats. This initiative has greatly enhanced the customer experience, allowing them to interact with technical documents in whichever format best suits their needs.


In addition to developing this platform, I utilize version control tools like GitHub and GitLab to manage and update documentation effectively. Working with these tools ensures that all document versions are consistent, up-to-date, and aligned with the latest software changes. I coordinate closely with development teams to track project progress, making timely updates to documentation as new features and modifications are implemented. This process not only maintains document accuracy but also helps to improve collaboration with technical teams, who rely on these resources for their own workflows.
